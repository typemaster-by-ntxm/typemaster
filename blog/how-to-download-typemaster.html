How to Download and Install TypeMaster

Get started with the world's most focused offline typing tutor. Follow this simple guide to get TypeMaster running on your PC in minutes.

You've decided to prioritize your typing flow and privacy. Great choice. Because TypeMaster is a native desktop application, it requires a quick download and installation. This ensures you get zero-latency performance and complete offline capability.

TypeMaster is completely free and available from multiple trusted sources. We recommend downloading directly from our official repository or verified mirrors.

Step 2: Verify System Requirements

TypeMaster is built with Rust, making it incredibly lightweight. However, ensure your system meets these basic requirements for the smoothest experience:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 only, not available for mac and linux.
  • RAM: 4GB (8GB Recommended).
  • Storage: At least 200MB of free space.
  • Connection: Internet required for download only; the app runs 100% offline.

Step 3: Installation Process (Windows)

Installing TypeMaster on Windows is straightforward. Follow these steps once your download is complete:

  1. Locate the TypeMaster-Setup.exe file in your Downloads folder.
  2. Double-click the file to launch the installer.
  3. If you see a "Windows protected your PC" popup (SmartScreen), click More info and then Run anyway. (Note: This appears because we are a new publisher; our software is 100% safe).
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation.
  5. TypeMaster will launch automatically once finished.

Step 4: Troubleshooting Common Issues

While we strive for a bug-free experience, here are solutions to common setup hurdles:

  • Download blocked? Some aggressive antivirus software may flag new .exe files. You can safely add an exception for TypeMaster.
  • Screen scaling issues? If the text looks too small or too large, try adjusting your system's display scale settings or maximizing the TypeMaster window.
  • Need an alternative? You can also find us on UpToDown.

Step 5: Start Your First Session

Once installed, open TypeMaster. You won't need to create an account or log in. simply select a Story or a Lesson and begin typing. Your progress is saved locally to your device automatically.
